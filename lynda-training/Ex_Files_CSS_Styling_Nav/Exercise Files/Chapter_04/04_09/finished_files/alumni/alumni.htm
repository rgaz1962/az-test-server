Alumni

Welcome back! The Alumni page is a place for graduates of the Roux Academy to stay current with what’s happening on campus, follow fellow alumni, and continue to be a part of the Roux Family.

For information about donating to the Roux Foundation, visit the Foundation website.

Alumni Profile

This month's Alumni Profile is Arnie Palmer, BFA Studio Concentration. During his recent visit to New York for his upcoming show he was generous enough to sit down with us and talk about his formative years as a student on the North campus and life in the outside world.

Read the full interview, and be sure to visit the Cheek Chastain Gallery site for information on Arnie's latest show. You can also explore Arnie's work on his site Futuristic Gumbo.
